About Loops...

I'm Julia, the creative mind behind this online haven for handmade lovers. From modern bags to cozy scarves, and trendy beanies to warm cardigans, our store is a celebration of the timeless art of crochet. With a passion for yarn and a hook in hand, I've embarked on a journey to bring both traditional techniques and contemporary designs back where they belong, "walking" down the street, or simply making homes feel like home. Each piece in our store is a labor of love, meticulously crafted to bring warmth, beauty, and a touch of handmade charm to your life.
